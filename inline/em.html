Itallic Text

The em inline element typesets chosen text in an itallic font, like this for example.

Semantic Considerations

When you want to emphasize something, you can use the em element. Or, if you want something withing your page to visually stand out.

Common Attributes and Values

When itallizicing text, it is important to remember to do so properly. Also you should consider how itallic text will look with certain fonts and typefaces on pages.
